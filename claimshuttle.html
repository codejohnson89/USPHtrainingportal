Claim Shuttle Verification Process

Made by Micah Johnson with Scribe



1. Click "Claim Shuttle"

2. Click "Log In"

3. Click "Send code via Email"

4. Click here

5. Check your email for the code sent to your inbox. (Check junk/spam box).

Copy (CTRL+C) the code and go back to Claim Shuttle.

6. Paste (CTRL + V) the code and click Submit.

7. Click here

8. Enter the First Name, Last Name, DOB, and Member ID/HICN.

9. Click on the drop down menu.

10. Select the first option.

11. Make sure in the Services section you ONLY have the 7 options shown below checked off.

Click Submit.

12. If an error occurs, click on the top section button FIX and it will return you back to the previous screen.

Skip this if your information is correct.

13. Click here to confirm to fix the information.

Skip this section if information is correct.

14. Once you enter the information correctly, you should search for ( CTRL+F) on the page and type in the input box Health Benefit Plan Coverage or scroll down to the page to it. There is two and the row we are going to use will be Part B.

15. Make note of amount in both the Deductible and Remaining amount. This info will be used to put into Raintree.

16. Return to Raintree.

17. Go to Patient Files

18. Search for the Patient.

19. Double click on the Patient's name and then select the Insurance/Payor option.

20. Select Payor

21. Enter Effective Date(s)

22. Click here to suppress Billing Until (the date will be a year from the current date).

Then enter the Sub ID for the insurance.

23. Scroll down to Eligibilty Records on the bottom right of the screen.

24. Right click here in the green box.

25. Select Add.

26. Click here and enter the current date for Verified On and the enter 01/01 for Policy limits reset on date below.

27. Click/scroll here to the bottom left of the screen

28. Enter the information from the website here with the current date for "as of" field and 01/01 for Policy Reset on field

29. Go back to Claim Shuttle website and scroll down or search (CTRL+F) for Physical Medicine - Medicare Part B.

Here we are going to check the current Benefit Year of {current year} and verifying the Amount column.

30. Input in the Limit field 2410 (this is the same for all Medicare Part B patients). For the Used outside FT field, enter the information from the previous screen of the Claim Shuttles website that's shown in the Amount column.

31. Click in the text area as shown below.

32. Enter the information as shown in the screen shot exactly how you see it here.

33. The effective date will always be 1/1/current year.

34. Click F10 to Save.

35. You would receive a pop up modal confirming either the record is "pending" or "verified".

Click Verified.

36. Click on Benefits tab.

37. Click Print Benefits info.

38. Press [[Ctrl]] + [[P]]

39. Press Enter on the keyboard or Print on the bottom left corner of the screen to print the doc.

40. Go back to Claim Shuttle website and scroll to the top of the page.

41. Click print button

42. Click "Print" again.

43. Click "Save". Make sure your printer option (highlighted area) is selected to Save as PDF.

44. Click "Save" and choose a location to save it.

Recommend to save the doc to your OneDrive folder in order to have access to it on another pc/laptop in the future.

45. Go back to Raintree and select the Insurance Cards tab.

46. Hover the mouse in the right area for the menu options to pop out. Click on the folder option.

47. Find the location of the document you just saved.

48. Click on the doc you just saved.

49. Click "Open"

50. Click "OK"

51. Press [[F10]]
